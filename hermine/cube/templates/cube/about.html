Objectives

Hermine is a Free and Open Source project to ease legal compliance with third party software included in your developements. It provides a framework to organize your interpretation of the different Open Source licenses and their application to the SBOMs of your software products.

It is aimed to interface with and complement existing Open Source tools, like OSS Review Toolkit.

The Third party components we directly rely on
